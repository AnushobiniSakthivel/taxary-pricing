Multi-currency available as add-on for Starter (₹299/mo) or included in Growth+ plans. Real-time USD, EUR, GBP rates.
Why unique
Pay only if you need multi-currency. Zoho forces Professional tier (2x cost) for the same feature.
Impact
Start affordable, add multi-currency only when your business needs it. Perfect for gradual international expansion.

Complete Stock & Returns Management

Unlimited returns processing with bulk import (500 Starter, 1000 Growth), stock tracking, and complete audit trails.
