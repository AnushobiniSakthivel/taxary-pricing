Unique Differentiators vs. Competitors

1

Native Coupon Hub for Promotions

What it isCreate and manage discount coupons directly within the invoicing workflow.
Why uniqueNo competitor in the Indian SME space (Vyapar/myBillBook) offers a native promotion engine. Zoho requires complex custom plugins.
ImpactRetail and digital SMEs can run structured marketing campaigns without external tools.
2

Global-Ready Multi-Currency (Mid-Tier)

What it isProfessional multi-currency support (USD, EUR, GBP) included from the Growth plan.
Why uniqueVyapar is strictly INR-only. Zoho Books walls multi-currency behind its high-end Enterprise plans ($9+). We include it at $5.
ImpactService exporters get enterprise-grade international billing at SME price points.
3

Aalee AI Ecosystem Integration

What it isTaxary connects with Aalee Assist (AI Support) for unified business intelligence.
Why uniqueStandalone tools (Zoho/Vyapar) don't connect billing data to automated customer support or cross-product AI agents.
ImpactAutonomous business operations where support and finance intelligence share data.
4

Genuine Free Access — No Time Walls

What it isReal access to core invoicing with meaningful limits (10 invoices).
Why uniqueVyapar has no free plan (timed trial only). Taxary Starter is free forever for micro-users with digital-first features.
ImpactZero acquisition friction. Evaluate the product with real data for as long as needed.
5

Role-Based Access at SME Pricing

What it isGranular per-module permission controls for staff members.
Why uniqueVyapar offers very basic user control. Zoho walls granular permissions behind enterprise tiers. Taxary simplifies this for Scale teams ($10).
ImpactBusiness owners can safely delegate finance and stock ops without sensitive exposure.
6

Quotation → Invoice Integrated Lifecycle

What it isInstant 1-click conversion with tax, line item, and party data synchronization.
Why uniqueMost SME tools treat quotations as side-documents. Taxary ensures they are part of a unified, high-speed conversion funnel.
ImpactDramatic reduction in deal-to-cash friction for sales-led organizations.
