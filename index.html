Unique Differentiators

1
Genuine Free Trial — No Feature Walls
What it is
Spark plan provides real access to core invoicing with meaningful limits (10 invoices, 5 parties), not a demo or 14-day expiry.
Why unique
Most competitors (Vyapar, myBillBook, Zoho) either don't have a free plan or offer only a time-limited trial. Spark is free forever.
Impact
Users can evaluate the product with real data before committing. Zero acquisition friction.
2
Multi-Currency Invoicing from Day One
What it is
Generate invoices in USD, EUR, GBP, or any supported currency from the Pulse plan. No enterprise upgrade needed.
Why unique
Competitors like Vyapar and myBillBook are INR-only. Zoho Books charges ₹749/mo+ for multi-currency. We include it at ₹249/mo.
Impact
Service businesses billing international clients get enterprise-grade invoicing at SME pricing.
3
Quotation → Invoice in One Click
What it is
Convert any approved quotation directly into a sales invoice with a single click — all line items, taxes, and customer details pre-filled.
Why unique
Most tools treat quotations and invoices as separate workflows. Re-entering data wastes time and introduces errors.
Impact
Faster deal-to-cash cycle. Zero data re-entry between sales quote and billing.
4
Built-in Coupon Hub for Promotions
What it is
Create, manage, and apply discount coupons directly within the invoicing workflow. Percentage or flat discounts with usage tracking.
Why unique
No competitor in the Indian SME billing space offers a native coupon/promotion engine. Businesses currently manage discounts manually.
Impact
Retail and e-commerce businesses can run structured promotions without external tools.
5
Integrated Expense Tracking with Receipt Upload
What it is
Track expenses by category, attach receipt images/documents, and maintain a complete expense ledger alongside invoicing.
Why unique
myBillBook and Vyapar focus on sales. Expense tracking with document upload is typically a Zoho Books-level feature.
Impact
Complete financial picture — income and expenses in one place. Reduces need for separate expense apps.
6
Purchase & Sales Returns Management
What it is
Dedicated workflows for both purchase returns (to suppliers) and sales returns (from customers) with automatic stock and payment adjustments.
Why unique
Most SME tools handle returns as manual credit notes. Taxary provides structured return flows with inventory sync.
Impact
Accurate stock levels and financial records after returns. No manual reconciliation needed.
7
Role-Based Access & Staff Management
What it is
Assign per-module permissions to team members. Control who can access invoicing, stock, expenses, reports, and settings independently.
Why unique
Competitors offer basic user management. Taxary provides granular, per-module permission controls at the Apex (₹499) tier — not enterprise-only.
Impact
Business owners can delegate operations safely without exposing sensitive financial data to all staff.
8
White-Label PDF Templates
What it is
Generate professional invoices and quotations with your company branding — logo, colors, and details. No "Powered by" watermark on paid plans.
Why unique
Many tools include their own branding on free/basic PDFs. Taxary removes all platform branding from the Pulse tier onwards.
Impact
Professional-looking documents that build client trust from day one of upgrading.
9
Full GST-Ready Compliance
What it is
All invoices support GSTIN, HSN/SAC codes, CGST/SGST/IGST breakdowns, and state-wise tax calculation out of the box.
Why unique
Built for Indian tax law from the ground up — not a Western tool retrofitted for GST.
Impact
Businesses stay compliant without manual tax calculation. Ready for GST filing preparation.
10
Aalee Ecosystem — Unified Business Platform
What it is
Taxary integrates with other Aalee products (Assist for AI customer support) to create a unified business operations platform.
Why unique
Standalone billing tools don't connect to customer support, CRM, or AI. Aalee provides a growing ecosystem of integrated products.
Impact
As the ecosystem grows, Citadel customers get seamless cross-product workflows without third-party integrations.

All features listed are currently available in the Aalee Taxary platform. Future capabilities will be added to plans as they launch.

Prices shown are per workspace. GST applicable as per Indian tax law.
